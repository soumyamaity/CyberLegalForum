About The Conference

Exploring the Frontiers of Cyber Law and Digital Forensics

Cyber Bidhi Sangam 2025 is a premier gathering of legal professionals, cybersecurity experts, and students dedicated to advancing the discourse on cyber law and digital forensics. This conference aims to foster a collaborative environment for sharing knowledge, discussing challenges, and shaping the future of our digital legal framework.
